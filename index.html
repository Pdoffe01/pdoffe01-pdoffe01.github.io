About me

My name is Pedro Costa and I am a visual content producer based in London. I started my carrer in photography: clicking and retouching. It did not take much time to realise that video offers even more elements to spread a message, so I decided to go deep into this video-producing world.

I am currently working for a video producing company based in Shoreditch area and I also do some freelance work creating visual content for small businesses. Mainly video content for social media channels.

I am an undergraduate student of (BA) Arts and Media Management at Birkbeck University.
